## Task Overview

Update CRM database with 12 new client contacts from last week's networking event. Add complete contact information, tag appropriately, and set follow-up reminders to maintain momentum from the event.

## Prerequisites

Before starting this task, ensure you have:
- [ ] Access to CRM system (Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.)
- [ ] List of 12 new contacts from networking event
- [ ] Business cards or contact information collected
- [ ] Notes from conversations at the event
- [ ] Authority to add contacts to CRM
- [ ] Understanding of CRM tagging conventions

---

## Step-by-Step Execution Plan

### Phase 1: Preparation (5 minutes)
1. **Gather all contact information**
   - Collect business cards from networking event
   - Review any notes taken during conversations
   - Organize contacts by priority or interest level
   - Identify any immediate follow-up needs

2. **Review CRM system**
   - Log into CRM
   - Check for any duplicate contacts
   - Review tagging conventions
   - Prepare template for consistent data entry

### Phase 2: Data Entry (12 minutes - ~1 min per contact)
3. **Add each contact to CRM**
   For each of the 12 contacts, enter:
   - **Name:** First and Last name
   - **Company:** Company name and industry
   - **Email:** Primary email address
   - **Phone:** Direct phone number
   - **Title/Role:** Job title and department
   - **Notes:** Key points from conversation
     - What they're interested in
     - Pain points mentioned
     - Potential opportunities
     - Personal details (hobbies, interests)

4. **Tag appropriately**
   - Add tag: "Networking Event - Jan 2026"
   - Add source: "Networking Event"
   - Add lead status: "New Lead" or "Prospect"
   - Add industry/category tags
   - Add interest level: Hot/Warm/Cold

### Phase 3: Follow-up Setup (3 minutes)
5. **Set follow-up reminders**
   - Schedule follow-up task for each contact
   - Timing: 3-5 days after event (this week)
   - Action: Send personalized follow-up email
   - Priority based on conversation quality

6. **Categorize by action needed**
   - Immediate follow-up (hot leads)
   - Standard follow-up (warm leads)
   - Long-term nurture (cold leads)
