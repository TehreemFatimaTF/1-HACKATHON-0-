### Phase 2: Invoice Creation (5 minutes)
3. **Generate invoice**
   - Open accounting software or invoice template
   - Enter invoice number (sequential)
   - Set invoice date: Today (2026-01-19)
   - Set due date: Net 30 or as per agreement
   - Input client information
   - Add line items with descriptions
   - Calculate subtotal, tax, and total ($2,500)

4. **Review for accuracy**
   - Verify all amounts are correct
   - Check client information is accurate

### Phase 3: Delivery (2 minutes)
5. **Send invoice to Client A**
   - Export invoice as PDF
   - Compose professional email
   - Attach invoice PDF
   - Include payment instructions
   - Send before end of business day

6. **Record in accounting system**
   - Mark invoice as sent
   - Set follow-up reminder for due date
   - Update accounts receivable
