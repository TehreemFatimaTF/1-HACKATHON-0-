## Step-by-Step Execution Plan

### Phase 1: Document Review (15 minutes)
1. **Locate and open the security audit report**
   - Check email for report attachment
   - Check shared drive/document management system
   - Verify you have the latest version

2. **Quick scan for critical findings**
   - Review executive summary first
   - Identify any "Critical" or "High" severity items
   - Note page numbers of key sections

3. **Deep dive into critical vulnerabilities**
   - Read detailed descriptions of each critical issue
   - Understand the potential impact
   - Review evidence/proof of concept

### Phase 2: Remediation Plan Review (10 minutes)
4. **Evaluate proposed remediation steps**
   - Check if remediation plans are realistic
   - Verify timelines are achievable
   - Ensure resources are allocated

5. **Assess risk mitigation**
   - Confirm critical issues are addressed
   - Check if temporary mitigations are in place
   - Verify no gaps in coverage

### Phase 3: Approval & Sign-off (5 minutes)
6. **Complete compliance documentation**
   - Sign audit acknowledgment form
   - Approve remediation plan
   - Document any concerns or conditions

7. **Submit to compliance team**
   - Email signed documents to compliance officer
   - Request confirmation of receipt
   - Set follow-up reminder for remediation tracking
