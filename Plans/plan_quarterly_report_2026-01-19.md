## Task Overview

Generate comprehensive Q4 2025 performance report for management review meeting. This report will provide insights into sales performance, revenue trends, and customer acquisition to inform strategic decisions.

**Business Impact:**

- Performance tracking and accountability
- Strategic planning for Q1 2026
- Stakeholder communication

Before starting this task, ensure you have:
- [ ] Access to sales data (Q3 and Q4 2025)

- [ ] Customer acquisition statistics
- [ ] Access to data visualization tools
- [ ] Report template or format guidelines
- [ ] Previous quarterly reports for reference

---

## Step-by-Step Execution Plan

### Phase 1: Data Collection (15 minutes)
1. **Gather Q4 2025 sales data**
   - Total sales revenue
   - Number of transactions
   - Average transaction value
   - Sales by channel (online, retail, B2B, etc.)
   - Geographic breakdown

2. **Collect Q3 2025 comparison data**
   - Same metrics as Q4 for comparison
   - Calculate quarter-over-quarter changes
   - Identify trends and patterns

3. **Compile revenue by product line**
   - Revenue per product category
   - Top performing products
   - Underperforming products
   - Profit margins by product line

4. **Extract customer acquisition data**
   - New customers acquired in Q4
   - Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
   - Customer lifetime value (CLV)
   - Retention rates
   - Churn analysis

### Phase 2: Analysis & Insights (15 minutes)
5. **Perform comparative analysis**
   - Q4 vs Q3 performance
   - Year-over-year comparison (Q4 2024 vs Q4 2025)
   - Identify growth areas
   - Highlight concerns or declines

6. **Develop key insights**
   - What drove performance changes?
   - Market trends observed
   - Competitive factors
   - Internal factors (campaigns, pricing, etc.)

7. **Formulate recommendations**
   - Strategic recommendations for Q1 2026
   - Areas for improvement
   - Opportunities to capitalize on
   - Risk mitigation strategies

### Phase 3: Report Creation (15 minutes)
8. **Create data visualizations**
   - Sales trend charts (line graphs)
   - Revenue breakdown (pie charts)
   - Product performance (bar charts)
   - Customer acquisition funnel
   - Geographic heat maps (if applicable)

9. **Write executive summary (2 pages)**
   - Key highlights and lowlights
   - Critical metrics at a glance
   - Top 3 insights
   - Primary recommendations

10. **Compile full report (15-20 pages)**
    - Cover page with Q4 2025 branding
    - Table of contents
    - Executive summary
    - Sales metrics section
    - Revenue analysis section
    - Customer acquisition section
    - Comparative analysis
    - Key insights and trends
    - Recommendations
    - Appendix with detailed data tables

11. **Format and polish**
    - Consistent formatting throughout
    - Professional design
    - Proofread for errors
    - Check all charts and tables
