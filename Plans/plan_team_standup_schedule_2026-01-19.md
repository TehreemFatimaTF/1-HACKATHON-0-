## Task Overview

Schedule weekly team standup meetings for next month. Set up recurring 15-minute daily standups for 8 team members, Monday through Friday at 9:30 AM, with Zoom link and calendar invites.

**Business Impact:**
- Team coordination and communication
- Daily alignment on priorities
- Quick issue resolution
- Improved team productivity
- Transparent progress tracking

Before starting this task, ensure you have:
- [ ] Access to calendar system (Google Calendar, Outlook, etc.)
- [ ] List of all 8 team members and their email addresses
- [ ] Zoom account with scheduling permissions
- [ ] Authority to schedule recurring meetings
- [ ] Team availability confirmed for 9:30 AM slot

## Step-by-Step Execution Plan

### Phase 1: Preparation (3 minutes)
1. **Gather team member information**
   - Compile list of all 8 team members
   - Verify email addresses
   - Check if anyone has known conflicts at 9:30 AM
   - Identify time zone considerations (if applicable)

2. **Create Zoom meeting link**
   - Log into Zoom account
   - Create recurring meeting
   - Set to repeat: Monday-Friday
   - Duration: 15 minutes
   - Enable waiting room (optional)
   - Copy meeting link and details

### Phase 2: Calendar Setup (5 minutes)
3. **Create recurring calendar event**
   - Open calendar application
   - Create new event: "Daily Team Standup"
   - Set time: 9:30 AM - 9:45 AM
   - Set recurrence: Monday-Friday
   - Start date: Next Monday
   - End date: One month from start (or ongoing)
   - Add Zoom link to meeting description

4. **Add meeting details**
   - Title: "Daily Team Standup"
   - Description: Include agenda and Zoom link
   - Location: Zoom (add link)
   - Add all 8 team members as required attendees
   - Set reminder: 5 minutes before

### Phase 3: Send Invites (2 minutes)
5. **Send calendar invitations**
   - Review all details one final time
   - Send invitations to all team members
   - Include brief note about standup format

6. **Confirm and document**
   - Save meeting details for reference
   - Add to team documentation
   - Notify team via Slack/Teams (optional)

## Potential Risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Team members have conflicts | Medium | Survey team for best time before scheduling |
| Zoom account issues | Low | Use alternative platform (Teams, Meet) |

**Escalation Path:**
- If time doesn't work for majority: Survey team for alternatives
- If technical issues: Use backup video platform
- If attendance issues: Escalate to team manager

## Standup Best Practices

**For Facilitator:**
- Start on time, every time
- Keep energy high and positive
- Enforce time limits
- Note action items and blockers
- Follow up on blockers after meeting

**For Team Members:**
- Prepare updates before meeting
- Be concise (1-2 minutes max)
- Focus on work, not excuses
- Raise blockers clearly
- Stay engaged during others' updates
