## Step-by-Step Execution Plan

### Phase 1: Research & Preparation (5 minutes)
1. **Review original email**
   - Read John Smith's inquiry carefully
   - Note specific areas of interest mentioned
   - Identify what they're proposing
   - Understand their company background

2. **Research TechStart Solutions**
   - Quick LinkedIn/website review
   - Company size and focus areas
   - Recent news or announcements
   - Potential synergies with our business

3. **Identify internal stakeholders**
   - Find business development team contact
   - Determine who should be involved in discussions
   - Check if anyone has prior relationship with TechStart

### Phase 2: Email Composition (8 minutes)
4. **Draft professional response**
   - Thank them for reaching out
   - Express genuine interest in collaboration
   - Acknowledge specific points from their email
   - Highlight potential synergies
   - Propose next steps

5. **Suggest meeting times**
   - Offer 3-4 specific time slots
   - Span across different days
   - Consider time zones if applicable
   - Suggest 30-60 minute initial call

6. **Add appropriate CCs**
   - CC business development team
   - CC any relevant stakeholders
   - BCC your manager (optional)

### Phase 3: Review & Send (2 minutes)
7. **Final review**
   - Proofread for errors
   - Check tone is professional and enthusiastic
   - Verify all recipients are correct
   - Ensure meeting times are accurate

8. **Send and track**
   - Send email
   - Set follow-up reminder (5 days)
   - Add to CRM or partnership tracking system
   - Brief business development team
